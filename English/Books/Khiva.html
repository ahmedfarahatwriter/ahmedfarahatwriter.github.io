Khiva

Publisher: Kotobna

ISBN: 9789776820029

"The algoritmi was proving day by day that he was the scientest that should truly hold the honour of this title, the title of a scientist, as if the language had been as serious as that title to be held by men as it was by algoritmi, a man dedicated to science as to doom." The journey of a genius child embarked on a scientific journey from the city of Khawarzam in Khorasan province until his arrival in Baghdad, the capital of science, in the era of the Abbasid State, to make his way to the skies of science flying above all the scientists of his time. The story of the extraordinary mathematician and astronomer Muhammad bin Musa al-Khawarzmi, a journey fraught with difficulties and dangers, in a period when science was developing enormously and needed men as much as algorithmic, a man of science who changed the history of science and if not for the delay of current scientific progress thousands of years.
