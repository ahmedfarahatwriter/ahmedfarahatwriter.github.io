Hi, I'm AHMED FARAHAT

Writer & Novelist

Ahmed Farhat: Egyptian writer and novelist "Member of the Union of Writers in Egypt". Born in 1983 in Cairo, he graduated from the Faculty of Commerce at Ain Shams University in 2009. He began writing in 2015, and was completed in 2017 with the book "History of Human Thought", published in 2018.

He began writing his first work of fiction "Khiva" in 2019. The first edition was released in 2020 at the Cairo International Book Fair. The novel qualified for the long list of the 2020 Sheikh Zayed Prize and was nominated for several other awards, including the International Prize for Arabic Fiction (Booker).

The youth representative, responsible for developing a practical vision for the advancement of culture at the 2023 conference "The Future of Culture in Egypt" under the auspices of the Egyptian Writers' Union. Participated in BA's Encyclopedia of the Heritage of Humanity for Young People with the book "Al-Khawarzmi: A Man Who Changed the History of Science."
