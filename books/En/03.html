The Virus Horror

Author: Ahmed Farahat

Genre: Adventure, Thriller, Science Fiction

Published: 2022

Description: In one of the virus laboratories in the city of Wuhan, a researcher named Chen experiences an accident inside the research room and tries to conceal the incident. However, he becomes infected with an unknown virus, inadvertently spreading an outbreak across the entire city of Wuhan. Renowned epidemiologist Wang Wei steps in to try to contain the spread of the virus, but the situation spirals out of control. The mysterious illness causes widespread fear and panic throughout the city, amid the silence and secrecy of the relevant authorities.
