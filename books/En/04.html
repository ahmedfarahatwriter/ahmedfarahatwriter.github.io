Journey to the Civil Regestry

Author: Ahmed Farahat

Genre: Satirical, Comedy, Thriller

Published: 2021

Description: A recent graduate is preparing to start his new job after finishing university. When it’s time to renew his ID card, he heads to the civil registry office to get a new one. There, he discovers that his information is missing from the records. This forces him to embark on a long journey to re-register his name and obtain his ID card. We follow Ahmed on his journey, sharing the adventures he faces in his quest to complete his official paperwork so he doesn’t miss out on the hard-earned job opportunity.
