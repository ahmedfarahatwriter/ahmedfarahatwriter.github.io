Ecology

Author: Ahmed Farahat

Genre: Adventure, Thriller, Science Fiction

Published: 2022

Description: After humanity survived a devastating pandemic that nearly wiped them out, they returned to their destructive practices against the environment. Gradually, living creatures began to disappear, and life on Earth faced the threat of extinction. A conflict erupts between scientists, on one side, and businessmen and economists, on the other.
Will science succeed in restoring what has been destroyed? Can humanity survive once again, or will greed lead them to their ultimate demise?
