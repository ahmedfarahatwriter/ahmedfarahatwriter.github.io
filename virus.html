Virus Horror | رعب الفيروس

ISBN: 9789779901688
Published: January 20, 2021 by كتبنا

In a virus lab in Wuhan, researcher Chen has an accident in the research room, and tries to hide the matter, but he gets infected with an unknown virus and causes an epidemic across the entire city of Wuhan. The famous epidemiologist (Wang Wei) appears to try to prevent the spread of the epidemic, but the matter gets out of control and the mysterious disease causes panic and panic throughout the city, amid the secrecy of the competent authorities.
* The novel was nominated for the Sheikh Zayed Literary Prize 2021
